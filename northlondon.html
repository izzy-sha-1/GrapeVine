I want to go to more plays!

A great north London theatre I've been to recently is the Almeida theatre in islington. A small theatre, located just off upperstreet, it has a huge kudos in theatre world. They do great ticket offers including 500 £5 tickets, available if you're under 25 for every play. And £15 ticket offers for the under 30's as well as a range of other deals if you live locally. I looking for a great pizza place...any suggestions?

Well if you have been London for any length of time, you may have come across the pizza chain Franco manca Originally starting as a single restaurant hidden away in Broxton it has now spread across London and you can find several newly opened Franco’s in Stoke Newington, Kentish town and Muswell Hill. For something a little more niche, why not head to the newly opened L’antica pizzeria da Michele –Only the second restaurant opened outside of its ative Naples and also Julia Roberts’ main love interest in the movie eat-pray-love, this pizza is Bellissimo!

I want to connect more with nature – are there any parks I could check out?

There are so many fabulous open spaces in North London just waiting to be explored. I can’t recommend Hampstead heath enough. It’s hilly terrain features ponds, woodland walks, wonderful views across London and even an English heritage House – Kenwood House – at one end. Also checkout Primrose Hill, and waterlow park at the top of Highgate hill is lovely spor.
